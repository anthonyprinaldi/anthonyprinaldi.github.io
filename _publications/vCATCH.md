This paper introduces vCATCH (volumetric clearing-assisted tissue click chemistry), enabling deep and homogeneous click labeling across the three-dimensional mammalian body. Combined with HYBRiD imaging and virtual reality visualization, the technique maps *in vivo* cellular targets of covalent cancer drugs afatinib and ibrutinib at single-cell resolution across whole adult mice.

Published in *Cell*, Volume 189, Issue 3, pp. 725–738, 2026.
